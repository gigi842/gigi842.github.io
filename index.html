My Story

14 years. Bad credit to 7 figures.

This isn't a highlight reel. This is the real thing.

When my mom had a stroke during my childhood, it changed everything. I knew early on that I wanted to build something that would let me care for her while still making a living — and help people just like us. So I set out to start a homecare business.

But nobody taught me about credit. Nobody taught me about money. When I tried to get funding for my business in my twenties, I was shut out — bad credit, no financial education, no access to capital. I was crushed.

Instead of giving up, I started reading. I educated myself on credit, finances, and investing. I learned that it all starts with mindset and execution. It took me 14 years of grinding, learning, and building — but today I'm a successful business owner making 7 figures, both online and in traditional business.

All because I fixed my credit, got my finances right, and learned simple investing strategies. Now I'm building Khori so your journey doesn't have to take as long as mine.

"If I figured it out with nothing — no connections, no handouts, no financial education — you can too. And I'm here to show you exactly how."
